Tex-Mex burger with cajun mayo

A burger with an extra kick:

A burger that has been supercharged with cajun spiced mayonnaise

to give you an experience you won't forget.

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon Cajun seasoning
  • 1 ⅓ pounds ground beef sirloin
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and chopped
  • ½ cup diced white onion
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 4 slices pepperjack cheese
  • 4 hamburger buns, split
  • 4 leaves lettuce
  • 4 slices tomato

Steps:

  1. Preheat your grill for medium-high heat. In a small bown, mix the

    mayo and 1 teaspoon of cajun seasoning and then set it aside.

  2. In a large bowl, mix together the ground sirloin, jalapeno pepper,

    onion, garlic, 1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning, and worcestershire sasuce

    using your hands. Divide into 4 balls, and flatten into patties.

  3. Lightly oil the grilling surface, and place the patties on the grill.

    Cool for about 5 minutes per side, or until well done. During the

    last 2 minutes, lay a slice of cheese on top of each patty. Spread the

    seasoned mayo onto the insides of the buns. Put burgers in the buns,

    and top with lettuce and tomato to serve.
