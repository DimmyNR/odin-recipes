Baked spaghetti

A tantalizing dish:

This is a dish that is easy to prepare and make at home. A versatile dish

that will suit just about any type of dinner and satisfy everyone's palate.

Ingredients:

  • 1 (16 ounce) package spaghetti
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 (32 ounce) jar meatless spaghetti sauce
  • ½ teaspoon seasoned salt
  • 2 eggs
  • ⅓ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 5 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 2 cups small curd cottage cheese
  • 5 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F or 175 degrees C. Lightly greade a

    9x13 inch baking dish.

  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook the spaghetti

    in the boiling water for 12 minutes until tender but firm to the bite

    before draining.

  3. Meanwhile, heat a large skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir the beef

    and onion in the hot skillet for 5-7 minutes until the meat has browned

    and the onion is soft and translucent. Drain excess oil from the pan;

    stir in spaghetti sauce and seasoned salt.

  4. Whisk the eggs, Parmesan, and butter in a large bowl. Add spaghetti and

    toss to coat.

  5. Place 1/2 of the spaghetti mixture in the prepared baking dish.

    Top with 1/2 of the cottage cheese, 2 cups mozzarella, and 1/2 of the

    meat sauce. Repeat layers. Cover with aluminum foil.

  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 40 minutes. Remove the foil and

    sprinkle with remaining 1 cup mozzarella. Continue to bake until

    cheese is melted and lightly browned, 20 to 25 minutes.
