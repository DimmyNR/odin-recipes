Lasagna

A homemade lasagna dish:

A lasagna recipe that can be prepared and cooked right at home.

With a juicy tomato sauce and cheese filling, It's a conventional

and straightforward lasagna dish that anyone can make and enjoy.

Ingredients:

  • ½ pound ground pork
  • ½ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 ½ teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ⅛ teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound small-curd cottage cheese
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 (16 ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps:

  1. Combine the pork and ground beef in a large skillet over a medium-high heat.

    Cook and stir for 5-7 minutes until it is browned and crumbly.

    Add onion and cook for another 5 minutes until transparent.

  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, garlic, basil,

    salt, oregano, and sugar. Reduce heat to medium-low and stir while it simmers for 30 minutes.

  3. While the sauce simmers, get a large pot of lightly salted water and bring it to a boil.

    Cook the lasagna noodles in the boiling water for 8-10 minutes, occasionally stirring until tender but

    still fairly firm when bitten. Drain the water and set the noodles aside.

  4. While the noodles are cooking, preheat the oven to 375 degrees F or 190 degrees C.

  5. Get another large bowl and mix the following: Cottage cheese, parmesan cheese, eggs,

    remaining 1 tablespoon of fresh parsley, salt, and pepper until fully mixed.

  6. Assemble the lasagna: Spread 1-2 spoons of sauce over the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish

    just to coat it. Place two layers of noodles over the sauce to cover. Layer with 1/2 of the

    cheese mix, 1/2 of the remaining sauce, and 1/2 of the mozzarella cheese.

    Repeat the process to make a second layer using the remaining noodles, cheese mix, sauce,

    and mozzarella cheese. Cover the whole baking dish with aluminium foil.

  7. Bake in the preheated over for 30-40 minutes. Then, take off the foil and bake the lasagna for

    another 5-10 minutes until the cheese is a golden brown colour.

  8. Remove from the oven and let stand for 10 minutes before cutting and serving.
