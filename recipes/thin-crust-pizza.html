Thin Crust Pizza

Description:

As the name would suggest, it's just a pizza with a flattened dough base. However, it is a very simple and easy to make dish that only requires common household ingredients and around 15 minutes of your time to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1/4 teaspoon white sugar
  • 3/4 cup lukewarm water
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour, divided
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Dissolve the yeast and sugar in warm water (44 degrees C) in a bowl. Let it sit until the yeast softens and starts to form a foam like substance (around 5-8 minutes).
  2. Combine 1 and 3/4 cups of flour and salt in a large bowl. Pour in the yeast mixture and mix it well until the dough forms into one substance.
  3. Lay the dough onto a foured surface and knead it until it is smooth. Then, add the remaining 1/4 cup of flour if the dough is too sticky.
  4. Roll the dough into a 12-inch circle before putting it on a greased pan.
  5. Top the crust with any toppings you desire and bake at 260 degrees C until it turns to a gold colour (around 8-10 minutes).
